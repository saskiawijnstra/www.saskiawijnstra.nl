Saskia Wijnstra

Design is my passion. I can make a clear creative solution from a messy idea. Designing creative solutions for customers and deliver a product where I'm proud of. That's what I love to do.

I'm born on July 14th 1996 in the Netherlands. From when I was little I liked to be creative. I studied Multimedia, Concepting and Design at the Hanzehogeschool in Groningen. In my third year of the study I went to Denmark to follow a specialization in UX design for six months. This was a great experience where I learned a lot. My graduation project for my bacholar was designing an app with Augmented Reality.

After my bacholar I got selected for the Master Digital Design in Amsterdam, where I graduated with a project for people with breast cancer to make them feel more in control. Together with only 24 international students I developed myself furter as a Designer through big projects, guest lectures from designers all over the world and much more.

When I'm not doing creative things, I'm doing sports. I practice aerial silks acrobatics (see video). Next to design that's also a big passion.

Now I am searching for a job! I would like to work for a company where I can show my creative ideas and design User Interfaces. Please contact me if you want to get to know me better.
