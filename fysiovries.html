FysioVries is a physiotherapy practice located in Vries. FysioVries strives for the best result for its customers. Quality, small scale, good service and personal attention to their customers are important for FysioVries. They offer this within a confidential and committed atmosphere.

I designed the logo and the website for FysioVries. I made all the photos on the website myself. The website had to be clear for young and old users. The most important information is on the home-page in the four boxes and on the other pages is in-depth text. I have built it as clear and clean as possible with fresh colors and photography.
