G-Force Capital is an investment fund for start-ups where 'ICT' or 'digital' is the core of their proposition. The G in G-Force stands for Groningen. The investment fund stands for honest, transparent, fast, clear, down-to-earth and northern. I tried to process this as much as possible in the logo and the website.

"G-Force doesn't invest in dreams, but in promising plans by promising entrepreneurs."

- Marco de Jong, G-Force
